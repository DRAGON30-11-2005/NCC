Dr. G. Vinay Kumar – Associate NCC Officer, Passionate Athlete, and Scholar


Dr. G. Vinay Kumar is a dedicated and passionate individual who exemplifies the values of discipline, leadership, and perseverance. As an Associate NCC Officer, he is committed to guiding and inspiring young minds through his remarkable leadership and dedication to the National Cadet Corps (NCC). His involvement in the NCC goes beyond simply providing instructions; he instills a sense of duty and responsibility in his cadets, fostering their personal and professional growth.

In addition to his work with the NCC, Dr. Kumar is an accomplished athlete with a deep love for sports. His passion for athletics has been a significant part of his life, and he constantly encourages others to embrace the importance of physical fitness and perseverance. His athletic pursuits are not only a testament to his personal dedication but also an example of his belief in the value of teamwork and resilience.

Dr. Kumar is not only a passionate leader and athlete but also a distinguished scholar. He holds a PhD in Genetics and Plant Breeding, showcasing his deep understanding of scientific research and his commitment to advancing knowledge in the field. His academic expertise and research contribute to the advancement of agricultural science, making him an invaluable asset to both the academic and research communities.

His multifaceted approach to life-blending his passion for athletics, his academic prowess, and his unwavering dedication to the NCC makes Dr. G. Vinay Kumar a true role model for those around him. Whether he’s guiding a group of young cadets, competing in athletic events, or researching genetic advancements, Dr. Kumar consistently demonstrates excellence and a tireless work ethic that inspire everyone he encounters.

OUR ACHIVEMENTS


Our cadets are distinguished in participating Thal Sainik Camps, Independence Day Camp, L. Republic Day Camps, National Integration Camps, All India Trekking Expedition, Advance Leadership Camps, Special National Integration Camps, Para Basic Camp, Army Attachment Camps, Parasailing Camp, Inter Group Competitions and along with the regular camps like Annual Training Camps and Combined Annual Training Camps.

Our cadets maintains the legacy of our college by achieving highest score in ‘B’ and ‘C’ certificate examination during Feb 2023
  • In ‘C’ examination CPL KATRAGADDA RENUKA CHOUDARY with the highest score 357/500.
  • In ‘B’ examination SGT DUPATI LAKSHMAN with the highest score 401.5/500.
Cadets also achieved best cadet awards, overall championships and individual championship in sports and culturals during the camps.
